Castle Wolfenstein 1981

Castle Wolfenstein is a stealth-based action-adventure shooter video game developed by Muse Software for the Apple II. It was released in 1981 and ported to MS-DOS, the Atari 8-bit family, and the Commodore 64. And though it gained numerous sequels it found a true redemption in the 2014 Wolfenstein: The New Order making it once again a massively successful game.
