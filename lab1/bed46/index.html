God of War 2005

God of War focuses on combo-based combat, achieved through the player's main weapon—the Blades of Chaos—and a secondary weapon acquired later in the game. Loosely based on Greek mythology, it is set in ancient Greece with vengeance as its central motif. It is eleventh best-selling PlayStation 2 game of all time. And while the game has remained relatively successful it received a reboot in 2018 presenting players an amazing new experience.
