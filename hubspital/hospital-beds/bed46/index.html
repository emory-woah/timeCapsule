Team Fortress 2 2007

Team Fortress 2 (TF2) is a team-based multiplayer first-person shooter video game developed and published by Valve Corporation. In Team Fortress 2, players join one of two teams comprising nine character classes, battling in a variety of game modes including capture the flag and king of the hill. While TF2 received critical acclaim as a breakthrough in its genre, it struggles to keep up with its many competitors making little to no new users as it proceeds.
